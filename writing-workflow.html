Writing Workflow

Monthly Cadence

We operate on a month-by-month cycle to simplify planning and start dates. For the current rollout, assume the cycle start date is November 5.

  • First meeting of the month: Pitch ideas and begin drafting projects.
  • Second meeting mid‑month: Cross‑team discussion to shape stories.
Phase 1 • Ideation & Prep
1

Pitch in Catalyst Tracker

A Catalyst writer submits an idea to the Catalyst Tracker. The pitch is reviewed and approved by Yair and Aidan.

2

Contact Interviews (CC Editors)

Writer reaches out to two potential interviewees for the article and CCs Yair and Aidan on the outreach.

3

Outline & Interview Prep

Writer drafts the outline and prepares interview questions. Submit both the draft and questions for review by a discipline writer.

Second Meeting (Mid‑Month)

Happens between these states. Writers meet within their groups to discuss and refine story direction.

Phase 2 • Interview & Editing
4

Interview & Draft

Interview is completed. Writer incorporates initial edits and shapes the story into a presentable draft.

5

Editor Review

Yair or Aidan reviews the draft and returns feedback to the writer.

6

Copy Edit & Assets

Copy editors finalize the piece: photos, citations, punctuation, grammar, and clarity. Prepare for publication.
